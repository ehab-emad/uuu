EDAG Workflow Browser is part of EDAG CatiaFramework Tool and allows user to view, manage, modify and further use Workflows. Workflows are template process descriptions with certain ammount of interactivity, which enables interactive and efficient way of solving/automising time consuming tasks. Workflows have pre-defined scope of definition and usage, they are composed of stages (process steps), objects (interactive results of process steps) and actions (activities that led to results).
